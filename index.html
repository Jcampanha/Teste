About Me

With over 15 years of professional dance experience, I've dedicated my life to the art of movement and expression. My journey began at the age of 5, and since then, I've performed on stages around the world, from intimate theaters to grand opera houses.

I specialize in contemporary dance, ballet, and modern choreography, creating performances that tell stories and evoke emotions. My work has been featured in international festivals and I've collaborated with renowned choreographers and dance companies.
